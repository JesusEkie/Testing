Grievance.app employs enterprise-grade security including encryption, routine backups, role-based permissions, and adherence to leading data protection standards.

What does the implementation fee include?

The $25,000 implementation covers deployment, configuration, data migration, branding, and administrator/end-user training to ensure a smooth launch.

Can we add integrations or modules later?

Absolutely. Add-on modules and third-party integrations can be added at any time, allowing you to expand capabilities as your program evolves.
