Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade plans later?

Yes. Your subscription can be adjusted at any time, and we apply prorated credits so you only pay for the time used at each tier.

Is there a free trial or demo available?

We provide guided demonstrations and a sandbox environment upon request so your stakeholders can experience the platform before committing.

How is our data secured within Grievance.app?

Grievance.app employs encryption in transit and at rest, frequent backups, and rigorous access controls that align with enterprise data protection standards.

What is included in the implementation fee?

The $25,000 onboarding package covers dedicated-cloud provisioning, configuration, data migration, branding, and comprehensive administrator/end-user training.

Can we add bespoke integrations for external systems?

Absolutely. Use the Third-Party System Integration add-on to scope, build, and support tailored connections to your existing technology ecosystem.

Do you offer support coverage around the clock?

Enterprise clients receive 24/7 priority support with the fastest response times, while Standard and Enhanced tiers include business-hours assistance.
